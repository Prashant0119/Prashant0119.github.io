About Me

I am an Aerospace Engineer with a strong passion for flight dynamics, control systems, and UAV development. My expertise includes aerodynamic analysis, stability evaluation, and computational simulations. I have extensive hands-on experience in UAV design, testing, and certification processes, having worked on projects ranging from nano-scale surveillance drones to large fixed-wing aircraft and eVTOL platforms.

Throughout my professional journey, I've contributed to DGCA-type certification processes, developed innovative telemetry systems, and performed complex CFD calculations. My background in both theoretical aerospace principles and practical implementation allows me to bridge the gap between design concepts and real-world flight performance.

I'm particularly interested in pushing the boundaries of UAV capabilities, especially in the areas of endurance, stability, and specialized applications like firefighting and high-altitude operations.
